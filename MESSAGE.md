For three years, I have been privileged to serve as part of this elite fleet. But as I look at the horizon, I realize that for me, the next stage of growth lies in the open water.

<br><br>

But why walk away from a role that represents a pinnacle for so many?

<br>

It’s not about leaving comfort behind; it’s about moving toward the frontier. I have a deep-seated need to experience this next era of engineering as a solo architect. I would rather take the risk of navigating these massive industry shifts head-on and independently than wonder "what if" from the safety of a structured harbor.

<br>

I value my autonomy and the ability to steer my own course so highly that I am ready to test my mettle. I believe — **now more than ever** — that a single engineer with a laptop, a connection, and a conviction can operate competitively on a global scale.

<br><br>

Thank you for the support, the autonomy, and the shared battles. Together, we’ve modernized this architecture and shifted the very landscape of Apollo Engineering. I take great pleasure in knowing that I have contributed to the changes that will carry this team forward.

<br>

I’m leaving my fingerprints on hundreds of technical documents, thousands of git commits, and millions of lines of code. But more importantly, I hope I’ve pushed the needle on what **Engineering Excellence** looks like.
Knowing that my approach might influence how you build tomorrow is more rewarding than any compensation package could ever be.

<br>

I have a strong conviction that value of engineering leadership and craftmanship is not contained by seniority or corporate titles. I believe our credibility is reflected in what we build and how we adapt, and I intend to spend this next chapter proving it.

<br>

The horizon is expanding, the very language of engineering is evolving, and the frontier of AI is calling. I’m heading out to meet it where the work is raw and the possibilities are endless.

<br>

It’s been an incredible ride, and I look forward to our paths crossing again in this shifting universe.
